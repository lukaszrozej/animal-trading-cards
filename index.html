Chameleon

Chameleons feed by projecting their tongues to capture prey located some distance away.

  • Scientific name: Chamaeleonidae
  • Tongue length: twice the body length
  • Tongue speed: up to 60mph
  • Tongue acceleration: up to 264g

Chameleons are a family of lizards. They have parrot-like zygodactylous feet, separately mobile and stereoscopic eyes, very long tongues they can flick out, a swaying gait, a prehensile tail, crests or horns on their strange-shaped heads, and many can change colour.
